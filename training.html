Training and Tutorials

Gateway ICT Consult and Advisory offers a wide range of training and tutorial services in various areas of information and communication technology (ICT)

Basic Computers

This training program is designed for individuals who are new to computers or have limited knowledge. It covers essential computer skills such as operating systems, file management, internet usage, email communication, and basic productivity tools like word processing, spreadsheets, and presentations.

Frameworks

This training focuses on specific software frameworks that are widely used in the industry. WordPress is a popular content management system (CMS) used for website development, while Odoo is an open-source enterprise resource planning (ERP) software suite. Participants learn how to utilize these frameworks effectively for website creation, customization, and management.

Programming Languages

This training provides comprehensive knowledge and practical skills in various programming languages. Java, C#, and PHP are widely used languages in software development. Participants learn the syntax, concepts, and best practices of these languages, as well as how to develop software applications and solve programming problems using them.

Software Training

This program focuses on training individuals in specific software applications or tools used in various industries. It could include software for graphic design (e.g., Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator), video editing (e.g., Adobe Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro), project management (e.g., Microsoft Project), data analysis (e.g., Microsoft Excel, SPSS), and more. The training helps participants become proficient in using these software tools for specific tasks.

COC Skill Gap

This training is designed specifically for Technical and Vocational Education and Training (TVET) students. It aims to bridge the gap between the skills acquired during formal education and the skills required by industries. The training focuses on enhancing practical skills, problem-solving abilities, and workplace readiness of TVET students, enabling them to effectively contribute to the workforce.

Research Software

This training caters to researchers and analysts who require software tools for data analysis, statistical modeling, and research purposes. SPSS, Weka, Matlab, Stata, and ODK are widely used in research and data analysis. Participants learn how to use these software tools to perform various statistical analyses, data visualization, and modeling techniques.

Overall, Gateway ICT Consult and Advisory offers a diverse range of training and tutorial programs that cover both foundational and advanced topics in ICT. These programs aim to equip individuals with the necessary skills and knowledge to thrive in the digital age and meet industry demands.
